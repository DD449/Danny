Also to include things like a survival crafting system as well which the player needs to build and craft and finally a coop or solo experience for people who want to bring the fun alone or together with friends.

I suggest this project will be rather interesting because after playing lots of video games from 2008 there have been tons of games that are fun to play but in the end most of these games always have a strong flaw to it. For example Minecraft has over 140 million players, while the game is fun in its aspect of survival, building and crafting mechanics it lacks a major flaw with progression, one you can be on the weakest tier of items then you can jump to like the best tier items after like 1 hour into the game this therefore leads its lack of excitement when playing which players tend to get bored and only have the fun of building. Description
The game I will be making is a work in progress so there is no proper name for it but let’s call it project X. This will be a game where it takes the most played genre of video games and combines those characteristics into 1, in order to make a game that interests a large player base. Looking at statistics in 2020 the top played gaming genres have been action games, adventure games , role playing games and coop/multiplayer. So the game will have to focus on being balanced with the genres being implemented, this means that all aspects of the game is perfectly balanced and isn’t one sided to what the game is good for, for example the adventuring story has an amazing story but the gameplay is rather lacking. By focusing on this I can build a service that can finally put gamers at a place where they don’t have to make the decision of “what to play” or “I have no games to play” at last. It will be challenging to make a perfect game but by gathering inspiration from other games that do well in its certain genres, this will take a lot of time to plan what is required to make the balanced game possible. The features of this game will be focusing on the story line, a good game isn’t a good game without a story to tell. By researching top games that have are known for its good story, I realised that most of the games are very cliff hangy which intrigues the player more, on top of that the protagonist of the story always has a strong emotional past and then following up with the Confidant (the protagonist close friend) that make the story even better, doing this make players have that connection within the character. The second feature we will be looking at is to focus on a ARPG (action role playing game) an role playing game is essentially in which players take roles of fictional characters, let say you play as the tank class, your role is to be the frontline of the team. Games like this bring a diversity to what players want to play, it’s kind of like a personality trait for what class you want to go. A healer can define the person as supportive and wants to help people, other than that most popular games like dark souls, terraria and final fantasy have the same thing in common, being rpg style games. To make a game with lots of diversity in its RPG style I would include around 10 classes to pick from so it would make the player feel like there is the right class and what feels right for them, basically every person should have a playstyle for the game. Thirdly, the final feature that will be included is to focus on how a building mechanic can work so that it also pushes the player to be as creative as possible in a different way where it’s almost impossible to have the same idea as someone else, including a defence mode that could challenge players to trial and error a strategy from time to time.
